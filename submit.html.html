Submit Your Music to NextGen Sessions

NextGen Sessions accepts submissions from emerging artists across hip-hop, grime, dancehall, UK rap and R&B. If your sound fits the culture, we’ll review it for future sessions, playlists and visual drops.

How to Submit

  • Send a private link (YouTube or SoundCloud)
  • Include your artist name and social handles
  • Add a short description of your sound or vibe

What We Look For

  • Original sound and clear artist identity
  • Clean audio (no clipping or distortion)
  • Strong atmosphere or visual potential

Submissions are reviewed weekly. We reply if it’s a fit.

Where to Send

Submit via Instagram DM. If it fits, we’ll be in touch.
